The Field Operation
Codename: Field Op · The capstone
ObjectiveRun one real operation that uses every skill of the summer at once.

Briefing

This is the mission you’ll remember. A secret message, encrypted two ways, transmitted across real distance on a radio you built, logged, decoded, and confirmed — with a fragment passed by Morse over the ham radios for good measure. Codes, signals, hardware, and history, all firing together. Plan it like the operation it is.

Field Operations

  1. Write the message. Encrypt it with your one-time pad (Mission 01).
  2. Send it over the LoRa link, whose channel is itself AES-encrypted — two eras of secrecy stacked.
  3. Deploy to two points — across a park, or two hilltops. Set spreading factor for the distance.
  4. Transmit. Log RSSI and SNR for the link (Mission 02 & 04).
  5. Decode at the far end. Pass one fragment back by Morse on the ham radios (Mission 03). Write the after-action report.

Equipment

  • Both LoRa boards, charged + antennas
  • One-time pad key (two copies)
  • Ham radios, notebook, two operators

Debrief — mission complete when

  • The message arrives and decodes cleanly
  • Signal stats are logged
  • The after-action report is written
