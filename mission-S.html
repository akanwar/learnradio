The Useless Box
Codename: Side Quest · In the spirit of Shannon
ObjectiveBuild something gloriously pointless, exactly the way the patron would have wanted.

Briefing

Shannon built machines purely for delight — a juggling robot, a flame-throwing trumpet, and the famous useless box: flip its switch on, and a little arm reaches out to switch itself off. That’s its entire purpose. The MIT Museum keeps his whimsical contraptions because play and genius were the same thing to him. Slot this in whenever you need a pure-fun afternoon.

Field Operations

  1. Gather a hobby servo, a toggle switch, a battery, and a small box.
  2. Wire it so flipping the switch ON drives the servo arm to flip it back OFF.
  3. Decorate the box. Give the machine a name.
  4. Flip it on. Watch it refuse. Laugh.

Equipment

  • Hobby servo + toggle switch
  • Battery + small box
  • Glue, paint, imagination

Debrief — mission complete when

  • The arm turns itself off
  • It made someone laugh
  • You felt like Shannon for a minute
Handler note: There’s no Shannon “twist” here because the whole mission is the twist — the point of genius is that it can also just be fun.
